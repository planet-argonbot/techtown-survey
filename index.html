Portland Diversity in Tech

In 2015, over 25 tech companies in Portland signed a pledge to improve diversity in our industry. As part of that pledge, “5 Actions” were agreed upon that pledge companies will take in an effort to bring about change. The fifth action is “Share our collective data” on the demographic representation of our offices in the Portland metro area on the basis of race and gender.

As part of that action, a survey was distributed earlier this year, to gather baseline data on key demographics that will support our measurement of progress. The results are provided below, and will be essential in guiding meaningful work on diversity and inclusion initiatives, and to track our progress.

Our efforts will create a needed pool of talent for our industry and establish Portland as a place where everyone can have a fruitful career in technology. There is a long road ahead but we are proud to be united."
